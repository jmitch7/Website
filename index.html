New Mexico's Crazy Telescope

Okay, I was kind of expecting it, but it still blew me away. The VLA stands for Very Large Array. It was the first and remains one of the very few telescopes of its kind. In technical terms, the VLA captures centimeter-wavelength waves meaning that the apparatus operates differently to the telescopes that capture visible light that humans have adapted to see. The frequencies that the VLA capture are much longer than the frequencies of visible light, making the large, 26-mile potential diameter of antennae the best way to capture these wavelengths.
